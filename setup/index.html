My Desk Setup

Posted at — Aug 14, 2022

What is on my Desk?

I have always been a sucker for good desk setups, and I have wanted one ever since I saw MKBHD's youtube video. While my desk setup is not glamourous, it is functional, and I am happy with its current state. So, here we go!

  • Laptop: I use an Apple MacBook M1 Pro running macOS Sequoia as my personal driver. I bought this recently for my side gig.

  • Monitor: I use a BenQ 27 Inch FHD display. I bought this during an Amazon sale.

  • Keyboard: I use the Logitech K380 keyboard. I love this keyboard for its multi-device support. It supports a seamless switch between my personal laptop, work laptop, and tablet. Super functional.

  • Mouse: I use a basic Logitech M33 mouse. It gets the job done.

  • Speakers: I got an Amazon Echo Plus for winning a hackathon at Dell. I have been using it for over a year, and honestly, I use it for setting reminders and listening to music.

  • Earphones: I switch between Airpods Pro 2 (wireless) and KZ ZSN Pro (wired).

  • Table: I have always wanted a wide table and found this from the Facebook marketplace for a reasonable price.

  • Chair: One of the best purchases ever. I spend over 10 hours sitting in front of my computer every day, and a plastic chair was not sustainable. I shifted to a Featherlite Astro Ergonomic chair; honestly, it is the best purchase ever.

  • Tablet: I use a iPad Pro 11-inch for taking handwritten notes.

  • Mobile: I use a iPhone 13 as my daily driver.

What’s next?

While I am satisfied with my hardware, I am still working on my software. I am currently self-hosting applications for my daily use, and I will update this page shortly with more details regarding the same.
